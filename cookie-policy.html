Cookie Policy

A plain-language explanation of the limited preference storage used on this website.

On this page

  • Overview
  • Current storage
  • Your choice
  • Future changes
  • Contact

Effective July 20, 2026

1. Overview

Cookies and similar browser technologies can store small pieces of information. The current Verdeon website does not enable third-party advertising cookies or behavioral advertising profiles.

2. Current Browser Storage

The cookie preference component stores one localStorage value named verdeonCookieConsent:v1. It records whether you selected Accept or Decline so the notice does not reappear on every page.

This preference does not identify you and is not transmitted by the component to Verdeon. Hosting infrastructure may use essential security or delivery technologies outside the page interface.

3. Managing Your Choice

You can decline preference storage from the notice. You can also remove the value by clearing website data in your browser. Clearing it will cause the notice to appear again.

4. Future Changes

If Verdeon later introduces analytics or other non-essential technologies, this policy and the consent interface will be updated before those technologies are enabled where consent is required.

5. Contact

Questions about browser storage can be sent to support@verdeon.com.
