Effective July 20, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Verdeon Design Network LLC (“Verdeon,” “we,” or “us”) handles information when you visit verdeon.com, submit an inquiry, or communicate with us. Verdeon is an independent information and provider-matching platform and does not directly perform landscape construction or maintenance services.

2. Information We Collect

Information you submit

The general contact form may collect your full name, email address, inquiry type, landscape design interest, property type, project stage, planning budget range, message, consent choice, and source page. The form also uses a non-visible anti-spam field and submission timing value.

Technical information

Our hosting provider may automatically process limited server-log data such as IP address, browser type, requested page, timestamp, and security events. Verdeon does not use the current website to build advertising profiles.

3. How Information Is Used

  • To review and respond to inquiries.
  • To understand the requested landscape information, advertising opportunity, collaboration, or general question.
  • To request useful clarification.
  • To facilitate a requested introduction to an independent provider where appropriate and available.
  • To protect the website against spam, misuse, and security threats.
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations.

4. When Information May Be Shared

Verdeon may share relevant inquiry information with an independent professional or provider only when needed to facilitate a connection you requested or reasonably expected from your inquiry. We may also use hosting, email, security, and professional service providers that process information for Verdeon under appropriate obligations.

We may disclose information where required by law, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a business reorganization. Verdeon does not sell contact-form information.

5. Retention and Security

Information is retained only as long as reasonably needed for the inquiry, relationship, legal obligations, dispute resolution, and security. We use reasonable administrative and technical measures, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

6. Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of information associated with your inquiry, subject to legal and operational exceptions. You can also decline the website’s cookie preference storage; the notice may then reappear if browser storage is cleared.

Email support@verdeon.com with a privacy request. We may need to verify that the request relates to you.
